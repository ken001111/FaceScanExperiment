# ARKitScenes 47331963 — depth-source ablation (PROXY reference)

GeoSVR lidar-fork, 5 arms x 20k iters, paper-default dtu_mesh cfg,
bound_mode=camera_median, subdivide_max_num=2M. Scored 2026-07-10 with
`score_47331963_proxy.py`.

## Reference caveat (read first)

47331963 is **not in the ARKitScenes upsampling split** (no laser-rendered
highres_depth exists for it) and direct registration of the Faro laser mesh
failed (register_v3: self-tested 4DOF search, negative result — commit
900b909). The only available reference surface is the **on-device ARKit
`3dod_mesh`** (~cm-grade). All numbers below are therefore cross-method
*agreement with the ARKit reconstruction*, not laser accuracy — the same
caveat class as ken's 2DGS-proxy scoring. Rankings, not absolute values, are
the meaningful signal; the laser-accurate companion table is scene 41069021
(RESULTS_41069021.md).

Frame-alignment sanity: training LiDAR back-projection lands 24.1 mm median
from the ARKit mesh under OpenGL convention vs 451.8 mm under OpenCV — the
workdir and the 3dod mesh share a world frame; no registration applied.

## Protocol

- GT = 3M points sampled from `47331963_3dod_mesh.ply` (1.45M verts),
  traversal-region crop (within 3 m of camera path): keeps 90%.
- Recon = 1M points sampled from `mesh/tsdf/tsdf_fusion_post.ply`, fine ICP
  (2 cm cap), 100 mm support crop for accuracy; support_frac = kept fraction
  (fog indicator).
- Stats via pe_verify eval_common (mm). F-scores at 5/10/20/50 mm.

## Conclusions — scene 41069021 findings REPLICATE on an independent scene

1. **Metric anchoring works again**: all three LiDAR-anchored arms beat
   photometric-only on every completeness/F metric (comp median 86 -> 57-62,
   F@20 0.21 -> 0.25-0.27), and support_frac rises 0.55 -> ~0.7.
2. **Mono-alone is again harmful**: comp mean 382 vs 321 for nodepth (comp
   median 96 vs 86) — scale drift shrinks the scene; slightly better acc
   median (29.5) is the classic shrunken-but-locally-accurate signature.
3. **Fused ~ raw LiDAR again** (within ~2.5 mm acc / 12 mm comp mean / 2pp F):
   room scans are the LiDAR-optimal regime; no arena for fusion upside.
   The fused>lidar claim still must be demonstrated on face captures.
4. Proxy accuracy floor: acc medians (27-33 mm) sit at the ARKit mesh's own
   error grade, so accuracy differences between arms are compressed;
   completeness and F-scores carry the discrimination.
